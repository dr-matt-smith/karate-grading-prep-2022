# Syllabus for 2nd kyu (brown + 1 white) to 1st kyu (brown + 2 white)


sequence (starting with both left and right lefts)
- step forward
  - mei geri, landing double punch (snapping)
- step back
  - geden barai
  - gyakazuki - keep arm out straight
- step forward
  - kekomi (side kick)
  - back knuckle strike
- step forward
  - oi zuki (snapping punch)
